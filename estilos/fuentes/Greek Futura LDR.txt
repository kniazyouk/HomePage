
GREEK FUTURA LDR Font

Greek letters are coded both in Unicode ranges 370-3FF, 1F00-1FFF and in ASCII set.
In ASCII set Greek alphabet is to be typed with the following keys: 
A, B, G, D, E, Z, H, Q, I, K, L, M, N, C, O, P, R, S, T, U, F, X, Y, W.
A special sign for "kai" is obtainable with ampersand key.
